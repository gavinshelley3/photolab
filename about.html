Hello!!


       My name is Megan Crow and I'm a photographer from Arizona. I specialize in foot photography, and I absolutely love it! I've been taking photos for as long as I can remember, and it's always been one of my favorite things to do. I'm so passionate about photography and I truly believe that it's an art form that everyone can enjoy.

       I love capturing the beauty of the human body, and there's something about feet that I find particularly intriguing. I love exploring the different shapes, sizes, and colors of feet, and I strive to capture their uniqueness in every photo. I hope that my photos can inspire others to see the beauty in feet, and to appreciate them for the amazing things that they are.
